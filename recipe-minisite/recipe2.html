Red Velvet Cake

Prep Time: 20 Min
Cook Time: 30 Min
Total Time: 50 Min

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • Frosting: 8 oz cream cheese, ½ cup butter, 2 cups powdered sugar

Equipment:

  • 2 round cake pans (9-inch)
  • Mixing bowls
  • Whisk
  • Spatula
  • Electric mixer (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking soda, cocoa, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix eggs, oil, buttermilk, red food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  5. Divide batter evenly between pans and bake for 25–30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let cakes cool completely before frosting with cream cheese frosting.
