Homemade Pumpkin Pie

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook Time: 50 Min
Total Time: 1 Hr 5 Min

Ingredients:

  • 1 (15 oz) can pumpkin puree
  • 1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ginger
  • ½ tsp nutmeg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch pie crust

Equipment:

  • Mixing bowl
  • Whisk
  • 9-inch pie pan
  • Oven

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk pumpkin, condensed milk, eggs, and spices until smooth.
  3. Pour mixture into the pie crust.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce temperature to 350°F (175°C) and bake for another 35 minutes or until set.
  5. Cool for 2 hours before serving. Top with whipped cream if desired.
