How To Make Triple Chocolate Cookies

Prep Time: 15 Min
Cook Time: 45 Min
Total Time: 1 Hr

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅓ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ⅓ cup white chocolate chips
  • ⅓ cup milk chocolate chips

Equipment:

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Small mixing bowl
  • Spatula
  • Whisk
  • Parchment Paper
  • Wire Rack

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone mat.
  2. Mash the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl until creamy. Add egg and vanilla; whisk vigorously for 20–30 seconds.
  3. Whisk the flour, salt, and baking soda in a small bowl. Add to the butter mixture and mix just until combined. Fold in all chocolate chips. Scoop dough onto prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake for about 10 minutes until golden brown. Cool for 10 minutes on the sheet, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
