Maintaining Tree Health

Proper care involves attention to watering, sunlight, seasonal growth, and repotting timing. Each species responds differently, so understanding your tree's needs is essential.

Seasonal Care

Spring encourages strong growth, making it ideal for pruning. Summer requires careful watering. Fall prepares trees for dormancy. Winter care varies depending on whether your bonsai is indoors or outdoors.
