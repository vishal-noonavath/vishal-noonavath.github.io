Foundations of Bonsai

Bonsai begins with understanding the balance between horticulture and artistic design. Basic skills include pruning, wiring, root trimming, repotting, and shaping. These core techniques form the foundation for creating a healthy and aesthetically pleasing tree.

Choosing Your First Tree

It is recommended that beginners start with hardy species such as juniper, ficus, or Chinese elm. These trees tolerate frequent pruning and adapt well to shaping techniques. Starting with a resilient tree helps you learn skills without fear of damaging it.
