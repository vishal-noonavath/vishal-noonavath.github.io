Welcome to How to Bonsai

This site explores the beauty and technique behind bonsai creation and nurture. After nearly a decade of collecting and caring for bonsai myself, I created this site to share the fundamentals for maintaining these delicate living works of art, as well as my experience doing so.

What You Will Find Here

Whether you are new to bonsai or have years of experience, the site covers a range of topics including beginner essentials, shaping styles, proper tree care, tools to get started, and recommendtions.

Learning Through Practice

Bonsai is an art form that requires patience and attention. Each tree develops at its own pace, and the process is a collaboration between nature and the caretaker. This site highlights approachable ways to learn, experiment, and grow as an artist.
