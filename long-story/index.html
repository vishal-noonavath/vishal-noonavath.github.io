This is the story of a paper boat. Folded from a scrap of forgotten thoughts, it sets sail on a journey no one asked it to take.

It sails down a small stream with a swift current. The boat dances between rocks, weaves between aquatic vegetation, and ducks under branches finding a rhythm in the uncertain.

Soon, dark clouds start to gather. Rain pierces through the path and the boat begins to unravel. But still, it floats onward.

Suddenly, everything opens up. The stream spills into an ocean. The boat is small and there is no longer a path. But now it knows how to ride any wave.

Somewhere, the paper leaves behind its last ripple. The journey may be over but the story never dies...
